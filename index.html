BETI BACHAO BETI PADHAO

Eligibility:
1.The family should have a girl child below 10 years of age.
2.There should be a Sukanya Samriddhi Account or SSA, which has been opened in any Indian bank, in the name of the girl child in the family.
3.The girl child should be a resident Indian. NRI citizens do not possess eligibility for the BBBP scheme.

Eligibility:

SAKHI NIWAS YOJANA

Hostel facility is provided on rent under the Sakhi Niwas Yojana in different regions of India. Only working women and their children are allowed to stay in this hostel. Many women work in cities far away from their homes, for which this scheme has been started to provide security. Earlier this scheme was known as Working Women Hostel.

Learn More

NIRBHAYA SCHEME

States/ UTs/ Implementing Agencies. Under the Nirbhaya Fund, one of the schemes namely “One Stop Centre (OSC) Scheme” is implemented across the country since 1st April 2015. OSCs aim to facilitate women affected by violence with a range of integrated services under one roof such as Police facilitation, medical aid, providing legal and psycho-social counselling and temporary shelter.

Learn More

WOMEN HELPLINE SCHEME

The Scheme of Women Helpline is intended to provide 24 hours immediate and emergency response to women affected by violence through referral and information about women related government schemes programs across the country through a single uniform number.81 is a toll-free 24-hours telecom service made available to women affected by violence seeking support and information.
